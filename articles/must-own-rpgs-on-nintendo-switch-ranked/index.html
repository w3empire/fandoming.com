Top 21 Must-Own RPGs on Nintendo Switch Ranked

authorElliot "PixelProwler" Parker

Hey there, fellow gamers and Switch enthusiasts! Are you ready to dive into a world of epic quests, unforgettable characters, and hours of gameplay that’ll have you glued to your console?

Well, you’re in luck! Today, we’re ranking the top 21 must-own RPGs on the Nintendo Switch. Whether you’re into classic turn-based battles, action-packed adventures, or story-driven narratives, this list has got you covered.

So, let’s embark on this pixelated journey together!

21. Harvestella (2022)

Let’s kick off with “Harvestella.” Consider it a tribute to those who love farming and fantasy RPGs. This game effortlessly blends elements of daily life simulation with an enthralling fantasy storyline.

You’ll be busy taking care of your crops, making friends with locals, and discovering the world’s secrets.

The game’s seasons change, bringing unique challenges and events that keep the gameplay fresh.

Its vibrant world, charming characters, and engaging gameplay mechanics make it an absolute must-try for anyone who enjoys a slower-paced, yet deeply immersive RPG experience.

20. Bravely Default II (2021)

Next up is “Bravely Default II,” a masterpiece that harkens back to the golden age of RPGs. Its turn-based combat system is a nod to classic RPGs, but with a modern twist.

You’ll find yourself strategizing over the Brave and Default mechanics, which add a layer of depth to battles.

The story is a captivating tale of heroes and villains, with a world that begs to be explored. The art style? Simply gorgeous. It’s a game that respects its roots while carving out its own identity.

19. Tokyo Mirage Sessions (2020)

Imagine a world where the realms of Fire Emblem and modern-day Tokyo collide. That’s “Tokyo Mirage Sessions” for you.

This game is a hidden gem that brings together the best of both worlds, offering a unique blend of RPG mechanics and idol culture.

The battle system is flashy and fast-paced, and the storyline is as eccentric as it is engaging. If you’re looking for something that breaks the mold of traditional RPGs, this is your game.

18. Monster Hunter Stories 2: Wings of Ruin (2021)

Ah, “Monster Hunter Stories 2: Wings of Ruin.” This isn’t your typical Monster Hunter game. Here, you form bonds with monsters, exploring lush environments and engaging in a story-driven adventure.

The turn-based battles are a departure from the series’ usual gameplay but are executed brilliantly. The game shines in its ability to blend storytelling with the thrill of taming and fighting alongside various monsters.

It’s a fresh take on the Monster Hunter universe that’s both welcoming to newcomers and satisfying for veterans.

17. Valkyria Chronicles 4 (2018)

“Valkyria Chronicles 4” is a tactical RPG that stands out with its unique blend of turn-based strategy, RPG mechanics, and third-person shooter elements.

Set in a fictional version of World War II, the game tells a gripping story of war and camaraderie. Its hand-drawn art style is stunning, breathing life into a dark and tumultuous world.

The gameplay is challenging, rewarding strategic thinking and clever positioning. It’s a game that will tug at your heartstrings while testing your tactical prowess.

16. Tales of Vesperia (2019)

“Tales of Vesperia” is a title that deserves a spot in any RPG lover’s library. This game is a beautifully remastered version of the classic, known for its engaging story and memorable characters.

The real-time battle system keeps the action flowing, allowing for dynamic and exciting combat sequences.

The world is vast and begging to be explored, filled with secrets and side quests. It’s a timeless adventure that has aged like fine wine.

15. NEO: The World Ends with You (2021)

“NEO: The World Ends with You” takes you to the streets of Shibuya in a stylish and vibrant adventure. The game’s urban setting is a breath of fresh air, offering a different flavor of RPG experience.

Its combat system is fast-paced and requires quick thinking, blending physical attacks with psychic abilities.

The storyline is intriguing, with twists that keep you guessing. It’s a game that oozes style from every pore and is a must-play for anyone looking for something out of the ordinary.

14. Live Alive (2022)

“Live Alive” is an RPG experience unlike any other. This game is all about storytelling, presenting multiple narratives from different eras and perspectives.

Each story feels unique, with its own set of characters and gameplay mechanics. The game seamlessly shifts between genres, ensuring that no two chapters feel the same.

It’s a game that showcases the power of storytelling in video games, making it a standout title on the Switch.

13. Sea of Stars (2023)

“Sea of Stars” is what we call a classic RPG, capturing the essence of what made those games timeless. The game combines turn-based combat with a rich narrative, set in a world filled with lore and mystery.

Its pixel art is gorgeous, evoking nostalgia while delivering a modern gameplay experience. The game’s exploration and puzzle-solving elements add depth to the overall experience. It’s a game that pays homage to the past while charting its own course.

12. Fire Emblem: Three Houses (2019)

“Fire Emblem: Three Houses” is a strategic masterpiece. This game takes the beloved Fire Emblem formula and elevates it to new heights.

The story is deep and branching, with decisions that significantly impact the game’s world and characters. The tactical battles are challenging and satisfying, demanding careful planning and execution.

The game also introduces a school setting, adding a new layer of gameplay in managing and developing your characters. It’s a rich and engaging experience that’s hard to put down.

11. 13 Sentinels: Aegis Rim (2022)

“13 Sentinels: Aegis Rim” is a narrative-driven journey that masterfully weaves together the stories of 13 different characters.

The game’s mix of visual novel storytelling and real-time strategy battles creates a unique gameplay experience. Its sci-fi story is compelling and full of surprises, exploring themes of time travel and parallel universes.

The art style is stunning, with beautifully rendered backgrounds and character designs. It’s a game that’s as much a work of art as it is an engaging RPG.

10. Shin Megami Tensei V (2021)

“Shin Megami Tensei V” takes you to a post-apocalyptic world where gods and demons clash. The game’s turn-based combat is deep and strategic, with a heavy emphasis on exploiting enemy weaknesses.

The story is dark and thought-provoking, challenging players with moral dilemmas and philosophical questions.

The game’s atmosphere is hauntingly beautiful, with a world that feels both desolate and alive. It’s a title that’s not afraid to delve into darker themes, making it a standout RPG on the Switch.

9. Trials of Mana (2020)

“Trials of Mana” is a gorgeous remake of a beloved classic. The game updates the original with modern graphics and gameplay, while retaining the charm and essence of the original.

The real-time combat is fluid and exciting, and the ability to switch between characters on the fly adds a dynamic layer to battles.

The story is a classic tale of heroes versus evil, but it’s the game’s vibrant world and character development that really shine. It’s a delightful adventure that feels both nostalgic and fresh.

8. Persona 5 Royal (2022)

“Persona 5 Royal” is more than just an RPG; it’s a cultural phenomenon. The game combines traditional turn-based combat with life simulation elements, creating a unique and addictive gameplay loop.

The story is a deep dive into the psyche of its characters, exploring themes of rebellion and societal pressure. The art style is striking, with bold colors and stylish designs that set it apart from other games.

It’s a game that’s as much about building relationships and living life as it is about battling shadows in otherworldly realms.

7. Dark Souls: Remastered (2018)

“Dark Souls: Remastered” brings the legendary challenge and intricate world-building of Dark Souls to the Switch.

This game is notorious for its difficulty, but it’s the sense of accomplishment that comes with overcoming its challenges that makes it so rewarding.

The world is interconnected and expertly crafted, filled with secrets and lore. The combat is precise and unforgiving, demanding skill and patience from the player. It’s a game that tests your limits and rewards your perseverance.

6. Octopath Traveler II (2023)

“Octopath Traveler II” is a stunning JRPG that dazzles with its unique visual style, blending 2D characters with 3D environments.

The game tells the tales of eight distinct characters, each with their own story and motivations. The turn-based combat is strategic and satisfying, with a deep job system that allows for a variety of playstyles. The game’s world is rich and diverse, encouraging exploration and discovery.

5. Star Ocean The Second Story R (2023)

“Star Ocean The Second Story R” is a remaster of a classic that stands the test of time. This game is a sci-fi adventure that combines real-time combat with an expansive story.

The game’s character interactions and skill systems are deep, allowing for a high degree of customization and replayability.

The story takes you across different planets, each with its own culture and challenges. This game feels both familiar and fresh, a testament to the enduring appeal of the Star Ocean series.

4. Super Mario RPG (2023)

“Super Mario RPG” is an unexpected and delightful fusion of classic Mario charm with RPG mechanics. It’s a game that broke boundaries when it first came out, and this new iteration continues the tradition.

The game is accessible to players of all skill levels, with a combat system that’s easy to grasp but offers enough depth for those looking for a challenge.

The story is filled with humor and heart, showcasing Mario and friends in a whole new light. The blend of platforming elements with RPG gameplay is seamless, making it a unique experience on the Switch.

3. Ring Fit Adventure (2019)

“Ring Fit Adventure” takes the RPG genre in a direction that’s both innovative and healthy! It combines fitness and gaming in a way that’s genuinely fun and engaging.

The game cleverly uses exercise as a mechanic for progressing through its world and battles.

You’ll be exercising through squats, jogs, and stretches to beat a bunch of quirky enemies.

The story, while simple, is charming and serves as a great motivator. It’s an RPG that not only entertains but also encourages a healthier lifestyle.

2. Xenoblade Chronicles Definitive Edition (2020)

“Xenoblade Chronicles Definitive Edition” revitalizes a classic with stunning visuals and improved gameplay.

This game is an epic in every sense of the word, with a vast world that’s rich in lore and beauty. The real-time combat system is deep and engaging, encouraging strategic thinking and party synergy.

The story is a grand adventure of discovery, betrayal, and destiny, with characters that grow on you as the narrative unfolds. It’s a must-play for anyone who loves RPGs and is looking for an immersive and lengthy adventure.

1. Dragon Quest XI S: Echoes of an Elusive Age (2019)

Finally, at the top of our list is “Dragon Quest XI S: Echoes of an Elusive Age – Definitive Edition.” This game is a masterclass in traditional RPG design, delivering a timeless experience that’s both nostalgic and innovative.

The turn-based combat is as satisfying as ever, with enough variety to keep it interesting throughout the lengthy campaign.

The story is a heartwarming tale of heroism, friendship, and overcoming adversity. The game’s art style, inspired by the legendary Akira Toriyama, is vibrant and expressive, bringing the world and its inhabitants to life.

We can safely say that “Dragon Quest XI S” embodies the essence of the RPG genre, making it a must-have for any Switch owner.
