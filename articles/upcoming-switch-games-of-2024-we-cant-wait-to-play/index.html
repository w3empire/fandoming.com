Top 22 Upcoming Switch Games of 2024 We Can't Wait to Play

authorRyan "MysticMana" Kim

Welcome to our electrifying rundown of the “Top 22 Upcoming Switch Games of 2024 We Can’t Wait to Play.” Yes, you read that right - 2024 is going to be a bonanza of gaming goodness that’ll probably have us glued to our screens and joy-con controllers like never before.

Picture this: You’re cozied up on your couch, the smell of pizza in the air (because, let’s face it, gaming and pizza are the PB&J of the culinary world), and your Switch in hand, ready to dive into a world of adventure, mystery, and, of course, those odd games that make you go, “Who even thought of this?”

That’s right, 2024 is not just another year; it’s a portal to gaming nirvana, with titles so hot they’d make a dragon wanna retire.

Spoiler alert: Your social life is about to take a serious hit, but hey, who needs sunlight when you have a backlight, am I right? Let’s get gaming!

22Unicorn Overlord

Let’s kick off our list with a gem that’s got me super excited: “Unicorn Overlord.” Set to release on March 8, 2024, this game is shaping up to be something special. Here’s the scoop on why you should be as hyped as I am.

First off, “Unicorn Overlord” is the brainchild of ATLUS and Vanillaware, the same geniuses behind hits like “13 Sentinels: Aegis Rim” and “Odin Sphere”. That’s a pedigree that promises quality and innovative gameplay, right?

Now, the game itself? It’s a tactical RPG that’s all about overworld exploration and a fresh battle system, all wrapped up in that iconic Vanillaware style. I mean, we’re talking about traversing a vibrant world, assembling units, and directing them into beautifully animated battles.

How cool is that? You get to perform heroic deeds and grow your renown across five different nations.

The setting is a royal adventure where you fight against fate to reclaim your reign, alongside allies you can trust.

The whole concept gives me chills! It’s like stepping into a storybook with a twist of strategy and action. And with the game being presented at the September 2023 Nintendo Direct, you know it’s got that Nintendo seal of quality and excitement.

21Another Crab’s Treasure

Now, this isn’t just any underwater adventure. Imagine a world beneath the waves that’s a little quirky, a bit challenging, and totally engaging. That’s what we’re getting with this game, and I’m here to tell you why it’s making waves.

“Another Crab’s Treasure” is developed by Aggro Crab, known for their unique and often humorous approach to game design. Set for release in early 2024, this game is a blend of wackiness and adventure that promises a truly one-of-a-kind experience.

You play as Kril, a hermit crab with a mission. Picture this: your home (a.k.a. your shell) has been repossessed.

Yep, you heard that right! Now, you’re on an epic treasure hunt in a stunning underwater kingdom, but here’s the twist – you’re wearing trash as armor! It’s a literal case of one crab’s trash being another crab’s treasure.

This game isn’t just about the hunt; it’s about survival in an ocean filled with enemies way bigger than you.

The game’s world is intriguing, described as a “crumbling underwater kingdom.” There’s a mysterious curse, pollution, and you’ve even got the Duchess after you for neglecting to pay your property taxes on your shell – talk about underwater politics!

This setting provides a rich backdrop for an adventure that’s both humorous and thought-provoking.

20Picross S+

Are you ready to dive into a world of brain-teasing fun with “Picross S+”? This game is coming our way in 2024, and let me tell you, it’s shaping up to be a real treat for those who love a good challenge.

But what makes “Picross S+” stand out? This isn’t just a new Picross game; it’s a compilation of past Picross releases. Yes, you heard that right – it’s like a greatest hits album for Picross! This title includes all nine 3DS Picross e releases that Jupiter distributed via the 3DS eShop.

Imagine having all that puzzle goodness in one place, ready to play on your Switch.

For those new to Picross, here’s the deal: It’s a puzzle game where you use numbers as hints to complete illustrations.

It’s simple to learn but can be devilishly challenging. And with “Picross S+,” you’re not just getting a single game – you’re getting a remaster of the original Picross E game, with the other eight releases available as DLC.

This means a seemingly endless supply of puzzles to keep your brain buzzing.

19Princess Peach: Showtime

“Princess Peach: Showtime!” is set to hit the Switch on March 22, 2024. This game is all about our favorite Mushroom Kingdom royalty stepping into the limelight as the main protagonist. And let me tell you, it’s about time!

The big reveal came during the September 2023 Nintendo Direct, following a teaser earlier that June. The hype has been building ever since, and for a good reason.

We’re not just talking about a side character getting her own game; we’re talking about Princess Peach, a character who’s been a staple of the Mario universe since practically the beginning.

But what’s the game about? So, here is the deal: Peach receives an invitation to the Sparkle Theater. Sounds charming, right? But, as you might expect in the Mushroom Kingdom, things go sideways pretty quickly. Enter the main antagonist, the mysterious Wicked Grape, and his crew, the Sour Bunch. It seems they’ve got their sights set on causing trouble, and it’s up to Peach to save the day.

The setting of “Princess Peach: Showtime” is what really piques my interest. The game takes place in the Sparkle Theater, a location that’s both glamorous and fraught with danger. But Peach isn’t alone in this adventure.

She teams up with the theater’s guardian, Stella, to reclaim the spotlight and, more importantly, save the day. And here’s a fun twist: with Stella’s help, Peach can switch into different styles or modes, adding a fresh layer to gameplay and strategy.

This game marks the first time since “Super Princess Peach” (2005) that Peach is the star of her own show. It’s a big deal, not just for Peach fans, but for anyone who’s been following the Mario series over the years.

The focus on Peach in a leading role, especially in an action-adventure format, opens up a whole new realm of possibilities for storytelling and gameplay within the Mario universe.

18Cat Quest III

This latest installment in the award-winning Cat Quest series is set to bring even more adorable action RPG fun to your Switch. If you loved the previous games, you’re in for a real treat with this one. Let’s delve into what makes “Cat Quest III” a must-play on our list.

The setting of “Cat Quest III” is really cool. We’re talking about the Purribean archipelago, a place where Pi-rats are on the prowl, searching for a mythical treasure called the Northern Star.

You get to sail the seas in your own ship, and the world is yours to explore. You can visit various areas, meet interesting characters, and take on quests in any order you like. Talk about freedom and adventure!

This time, the game takes the cute and accessible action RPG to the high seas. That’s right, we’re not just roaming the lands; we’re navigating the waters. And if the idea of being a swashbuckling cat on a quest doesn’t excite you, I don’t know what will.

“Cat Quest III” is described as a 2.5D open-world action RPG, which sounds like it’s going to offer a visually stunning and immersive experience.

The game promises even more paw-some adventures, and from the looks of it, it’s going to deliver just that. Can you imagine roaming around a beautifully crafted world filled with furry friends and foes, all while unraveling the mysteries of the Purribean? Sign me up!

17Another Code: Recollection

Mystery fans, gather around because “Another Code: Recollection” is one title you won’t want to miss in 2024! This game is a treasure trove for those who love unraveling secrets and piecing together puzzles.

Set to release on January 19, 2024, “Another Code: Recollection” is a compilation of two enhanced mystery adventures from the acclaimed Another Code series. Let’s delve into why this game is a must-have for your Switch collection.

“Another Code: Recollection” takes us on a journey with Ashley, a 13-year-old girl searching for her father on the remote Blood Edward Island.

The story begins with Ashley receiving a letter from her father, whom she believed to be dead. This sets off a series of mysteries and visions into the past.

The game is a refreshed retelling of the original “Trace Memory” game for the Nintendo DS system, promising a blend of nostalgia and new experiences.

But the intrigue doesn’t stop there. The game also includes the events of the first game’s sequel, “Another Code: R – Journey into Lost Memories.” This installment completes Ashley’s tale as she heads to Lake Juliet two years after the events of the first game, on a mission to uncover the truth about her mother.

It’s a continuation that’s sure to enthrall players who have followed Ashley’s story from the beginning.

The game offers fully enhanced versions of both original games, featuring overhauled visuals and fully explorable environments.

Plus, there’s new voice acting, puzzles, music, and more to immerse players in Ashley’s world. For those new to adventure games, there are optional hint and navigation systems to guide you through the experience, making it accessible to a wider audience.

16The Gecko Gods

Prepare to embark on a unique and immersive journey with “The Gecko Gods,” coming to the Nintendo Switch in Spring 2024.

This game is all about exploration, puzzle-solving, and, well, geckos! Let’s take a closer look at what makes “The Gecko Gods” a must-play for 2024.

First and foremost, “The Gecko Gods” is a single-player adventure puzzle game that’s set to deliver a one-of-a-kind experience. It’s developed by Super Rare Originals and Inresin, promising a quality gaming adventure with a unique twist.

The game invites you to explore a mysterious island, but here’s the twist: you’re a tiny gecko! This isn’t your typical adventure game; it’s an immersive, relaxing exploration-adventure experience.

Imagine traversing a beautifully crafted island, climbing effortlessly and solving ancient puzzles. The game’s unique, immersive gameplay allows you to interact with the environment in a way that’s both innovative and captivating.

In “The Gecko Gods,” your mission is to save your friends, adding a sense of purpose and urgency to your exploration.

You’ll be exploring a set of mysterious islands and temples, each offering its own set of challenges and secrets to uncover. It’s a puzzle-platformer at its core, but with a relaxed, chill vibe that’s perfect for those times when you just want to unwind and explore at your own pace.

15Palia

Let’s dive into the charming world of “Palia,” a cozy community sim MMO that’s set to grace our consoles on December 14.

If you’re a fan of games that blend crafting, exploration, and community, “Palia” is an adventure you won’t want to miss. Here’s what makes this game a must-play on our list.

“Palia” is developed by Singularity 6, and it offers a free-to-play, immersive experience where you can craft, cook, fish, farm, and more. It’s an adventure game that provides a down-to-earth experience and the chance to immerse yourself in a world of friendly cooperation.

It’s not just about living off the land; it’s about building a community and unraveling mysteries.

The game is set in a vibrant new world where you can explore Kilima Valley and Bahari Bay, forage for items and ingredients, and discover rare critters. There’s an underlying deep mystery about what happened to ancient humans, and you, alongside other players, have a chance to unravel this enigma.

You can customize your character and build your dream home. With hundreds of craftable items and customizable options, you can decorate your plot and make your home uniquely yours. This level of personalization adds depth and a personal touch to your gaming experience.

Another thing that I like about this game is its focus on community and relationships. You can befriend the local Majiri, learn their backstories, foster relationships, and even delve into romance.

The game encourages you to give back to the community by honing your skills in various activities like fishing, hunting, and crafting.

And the best part? You can enjoy this adventure either solo or with friends.

14Contra: Operation Galuga

Action game enthusiasts, gear up for “Contra: Operation Galuga,” a thrilling revival of the legendary Contra series, set to explode onto the Nintendo Switch in early 2024.

This game isn’t just a return; it’s a complete reimagining of the classic run-’n’-gun action from the ’80s, and it’s packing a serious punch.

“Contra: Operation Galuga” is developed by WayForward, known for breathing new life into classic titles. This game promises to retain the essence of the original Contra while injecting modern graphics, sound, and new gameplay mechanics.

It’s a perfect blend of nostalgia and innovation, offering something fresh yet familiar to fans of the series.

The storyline takes you to the Galuga Islands off the coast of New Zealand, where the Red Falcon terrorist group has taken control.

You’ll step into the boots of elite Contra commandos Bill Rizer and Lance Bean, tasked with initiating an all-out war to save mankind. This narrative adds an exciting layer to the game, as players will run, jump, and shoot their way through eight levels, using a devastating arsenal of weapons including the spread shot, laser, and homing missiles.

And these weapons come with stackable upgrades and Overload abilities, adding depth to the gameplay.

The game boasts eight explosive stages, each with its own unique challenges and environments. From infiltrating a jungle to speeding through an enemy base on a hovercycle, every level promises high-octane action.

Also, the game offers multiple playable characters, each with distinct abilities, enhancing the replay value and offering different gameplay experiences.

Epic boss battles are a hallmark of the Contra series, and “Contra: Operation Galuga” doesn’t disappoint. The game features screen-filling bosses that will test your skills and strategy.

Plus, with new and fan-favorite weapons, the combat experience is more thrilling than ever. The game also caters to players of all skill levels, with multiple difficulty options and a wealth of extra content to explore.

13Test Drive Unlimited Solar Crown

“Test Drive Unlimited Solar Crown” is cruising onto the Nintendo Switch in 2024, and it’s shaping up to be a game that’ll redefine open-world racing.

Developed by KT Racing and published by Nacon, this title is poised to take the racing genre to new heights. Let’s break down why this game is racing up our list of must-plays.

“Test Drive Unlimited Solar Crown” is the latest installment in the long-running Test Drive series, and it’s the first title since 2012’s “Ferrari Racing Legends.”

This game represents the third game in the franchise’s Unlimited reboot, following 2011’s “Test Drive Unlimited 2.” For fans of the series, this is a significant return, bringing back the excitement and open-world exploration that the series is known for.

But what’s really exciting about “Test Drive Unlimited Solar Crown” is its setting. The game features a 1:1 scale recreation of Hong Kong Island, offering a vast and vibrant open world to explore.

With 550 kilometers (340 miles) of drivable roads, the game promises an unprecedented level of realism and detail in its world design.

This setting marks a shift in the series, as the game will feature left-hand traffic, a first for the series since “The Brotherhood of Speed’s” Tokyo and London settings. The attention to detail and commitment to creating an authentic, immersive world is a massive draw for racing enthusiasts.

12FANTASY LIFE i: The Girl Who Steals Time

As we continue our list of eagerly awaited Switch games for 2024, let’s turn our attention to “Fantasy Life I: The Girl Who Steals Time.”

This game, developed by Level-5, is a delightful sequel to the hit 3DS game “Fantasy Life,” and it’s set to launch on the Nintendo Switch in Summer 2024. Here’s why this game is generating so much excitement.

“Fantasy Life I: The Girl Who Steals Time” promises to be a rich and expansive simulation game that combines elements of farming, monster hunting, adventuring, and more.

This blend of gameplay elements offers a diverse and engaging experience, appealing to fans of various game genres.

The game has been delayed to ensure its quality, indicating Level-5’s commitment to delivering an exceptional gaming experience. Originally scheduled for a 2023 release, the decision to push back the release to 2024 was made to enhance the game further and provide players with the best possible version.

The anticipation for “Fantasy Life I: The Girl Who Steals Time” is heightened by the fact that it will have a worldwide simultaneous release. This global launch strategy underscores the game’s broad appeal and the strong fanbase that the “Fantasy Life” series has garnered over the years.

While specific details about the game’s plot and features are still under wraps, the excitement around its release is palpable. The success of the original “Fantasy Life” on the 3DS sets a high bar, and this sequel is expected to build upon the charming and immersive world that fans have come to love.

11The Legend of Heroes: Trails Through Daybreak

“The Legend of Heroes: Trails Through Daybreak” is the latest installment in the renowned “Trails” series, part of the larger “The Legend of Heroes” franchise, and it promises to deliver an epic role-playing experience.

“Trails Through Daybreak” is developed by Nihon Falcom, a studio renowned for its rich and immersive RPGs. The game represents a new chapter in the “Trails” series and is the first to be set in the Calvard Republic.

You’ll step into the shoes of Van Arkride, a young man working as a Spriggan, a bounty hunting detective. This setting and character role promise a fresh and engaging narrative, different from previous installments.

The gameplay follows a chapter format, where you spend each day in the city of Edith, interacting with characters and completing side missions.

As the story progresses, you’ll travel to new locations and welcome new members to your party. “Trails Through Daybreak” has an incredible dynamic world with no load times for entering or exiting buildings, offering a seamless and expansive overworld experience.

Another exciting aspect is the game’s combat system, which blends real-time action battles similar to Falcom’s “Ys” series with traditional turn-based combat.

This innovative approach allows players to switch between combat modes, adding a strategic layer to battles. Boss fights remain exclusively turn-based, ensuring that classic RPG combat fans are well catered to.

10Metal Slug Tactics

“Metal Slug Tactics” is the tenth spin-off in the Metal Slug franchise, and it’s designed as an RPG roguelike.

This genre shift is a significant departure from the traditional run-and-gun gameplay Metal Slug is known for. It’s an exciting evolution, promising to offer a new and unique experience to both long-time fans and newcomers to the series.

The game is described as a tactical spin-off, where players will engage in turn-based skirmishes shown from an isometric perspective.

This approach to gameplay combines strategic depth with the action-packed elements the series is famous for. The use of the brand’s characteristic pixel art style ensures that, despite the shift in genre, “Metal Slug Tactics” retains the charm and appeal of the original games.

In their commitment to creating a game that lives up to the Metal Slug legacy and meets player expectations, the developers have decided to release “Metal Slug Tactics” (maybe?) in 2024.

This decision reflects the developers’ dedication to quality and their desire to deliver a game that both honors the franchise’s history and offers something new and exciting.

Currently, “Metal Slug Tactics” is announced for release on PC and Nintendo Switch, making it accessible to a wide range of players. The availability on the Switch platform, in particular, highlights the game’s appeal as a title that can be enjoyed both at home and on the go.

9Star Wars: Dark Forces Remastered

“Star Wars: Dark Forces” is making a grand return with a remaster set to launch on February 28, 2024.

Developed by Nightdive Studios in collaboration with Lucasfilm Games, this remaster of the classic first-person shooter (FPS), originally released in 1995, is eagerly awaited by both long-time fans and newcomers to the Star Wars gaming universe.

“Star Wars: Dark Forces” is renowned for being the first FPS game in the Star Wars franchise. This game introduced a new way to experience the Star Wars universe, offering players an immersive and action-packed adventure.

The remaster promises to bring this classic experience to modern platforms while preserving the essence of the original game.

Nightdive Studios have a track record of faithfully bringing classic games to life with updated visuals and gameplay while maintaining the soul of the original titles.

This approach ensures that “Star Wars: Dark Forces Remaster” will not only appeal to nostalgic fans but also attract a new generation of players with its enhanced features and modernized gameplay.

8Prince of Persia: The Lost Crown

The legendary “Prince of Persia” series is making a grand comeback with “Prince of Persia: The Lost Crown,” set to release on January 18, 2024.

Developed and published by Ubisoft, this game is the first major installment in the series since 2010’s “Prince of Persia: The Forgotten Sands.”

“Prince of Persia: The Lost Crown” represents a significant evolution in the series. It is a 2.5D side-scrolling action-adventure platform game, bringing a fresh perspective to the classic franchise.

The game features a new protagonist, Sargon, who possesses the agility and combat prowess typical of the series’ heroes. Players will navigate through the game’s world, using Sargon’s abilities to jump, slide, and air dash between platforms, as well as engage in combat using a pair of blades.

One thing about this game that stands out for me is Sargon’s unique abilities, including time-based powers that add a strategic element to both combat and platforming.

For instance, the “Rush of the Simurgh” allows Sargon to instantly dash forward through time, while the “Shadow of the Simurgh” creates a temporary checkpoint. These abilities, along with talismans that alter Sargon’s capabilities, offer dynamic and varied gameplay mechanics.

Incorporating elements commonly found in Metroidvania video games, “The Lost Crown” includes an interconnected game world filled with shortcuts, secret rooms, and various puzzles.

This design encourages exploration and problem-solving, enriching the player’s experience. Time crystals scattered throughout the world can be collected to purchase new upgrades for Sargon’s weapons, adding another layer of depth to the game.

The game also allows players to pin screenshots to the map, helping them keep track of unsolved puzzles and areas to revisit, enhancing the exploratory aspect.

7Kingdom Come: Deliverance

“Kingdom Come: Deliverance” is finally making its way to the Nintendo Switch in early 2024! This medieval action RPG, first launched in 2018, is getting a royal treatment with its Switch port, and let me tell you, it’s been worth the wait.

The Switch port of “Kingdom Come: Deliverance” is being handled by Saber Interactive. These are the same folks who worked magic on “The Witcher III: Wild Hunt” for the hybrid console.

So, we’ve got some solid hands on deck here, promising a quality port that should hopefully iron out any performance kinks from the original releases on PS4, Xbox One, and PC.

“Kingdom Come: Deliverance” is set in the year 1403, during the rule of King Wenceslaus IV in Bohemia. You step into the shoes of Henry, a blacksmith’s son, whose life turns upside down when the war knocks on his doorstep.

This game is big on realism – think one-on-one combat, the need to maintain your hygiene, manage hunger and even deal with the aftermath of a wild night out. Yep, it’s like stepping into a time machine, minus the risk of actual bodily harm!

“Kingdom Come: Deliverance” comes packed with features that make it perfect for a portable gaming experience on the Switch. We’re talking about a massive, realistic open world filled with majestic castles, vast fields, and lush forests.

The story is non-linear, allowing you to solve quests in multiple ways and face the consequences of your decisions. Combat is challenging and rewarding, with options for distance, stealth, or melee engagements.

And character development? It’s all about improving skills, earning perks, and upgrading your equipment.

But here’s the royal icing on the cake: the Switch version will be the “Royal Edition,” including all the DLCs released for the game.

That means “Treasures of the Past,” “From the Ashes,” “The Amorous Adventures of Bold Sir Hans Capon,” “Band of Bastards,” and “A Woman’s Lot” will be available from day one.

So, not only are you getting the base game, but you’re also getting a treasure trove of additional content to enhance your medieval escapades.

6Eiyuden Chronicle: Hundred Heroes

“Eiyuden Chronicle: Hundred Heroes” is coming to the Nintendo Switch on April 23, 2024, and it’s already stirring up quite the excitement.

Developed by Rabbit and Bear Studios and published by 505 Games, this game is being led by Yoshitaka Murayama, the creator of the acclaimed Suikoden series, and is touted as its spiritual successor.

First off, the lineage of this game is impressive. If you’re a fan of the Suikoden series, then you’re in for a treat.

Murayama’s involvement ensures that “Eiyuden Chronicle: Hundred Heroes” will have the same depth and narrative richness that made Suikoden a hit.

The game’s development is the result of a wildly successful Kickstarter campaign, which met its base funding requirements within hours and raised over $4.5 million, making it the third highest funded video game in Kickstarter history.

This level of support speaks volumes about the community’s trust and excitement for the project.

As for the game itself, it’s an RPG that promises a massive and detailed world. The game features traditional turn-based combat and a story that unfolds in a vast, dynamic world.

While details are still emerging, the anticipation is built around the promise of an epic narrative, strategic gameplay, and a world rich in lore and character.

What’s particularly exciting about “Eiyuden Chronicle: Hundred Heroes” is its approach to world-building and storytelling.

Given Murayama’s track record with the Suikoden series, players can expect a game that’s not just about battles and quests, but also about intricate narratives, deep character development, and a world that feels alive and immersive.

5Little Nightmares 3

Get ready for a spooky treat, as “Little Nightmares III” is set to haunt the Nintendo Switch and other platforms in 2024.

This stand-alone sequel to the first two games is developed by Supermassive Games and promises a fresh yet chilling experience. Follow two new protagonists, Low and Alone, through the eerie 2.5D world of The Nowhere, solving puzzles and facing off against threats with unique gameplay mechanics.

What’s thrilling about this sequel is the inclusion of online co-op, a first for the series, allowing players to venture into the dark together or alongside an AI companion.

Each character brings distinct abilities to the table, enhancing the strategy and depth of gameplay. Unveiled at Gamescom’s Opening Night Live, the game marks a new chapter for the series, developed by a team well-versed in the franchise’s eerie essence.

4Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door

“Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door” is renowned for its engaging story, unique papercraft art style, and inventive RPG mechanics.

The Switch remake is not only bringing back this acclaimed game but also enhancing it with updated graphics. Imagine experiencing the adventures of Mario and friends with a modern visual twist, all while diving into the quest to uncover the secrets behind the ancient Thousand-Year Door.

This remake marks the first in the Paper Mario series, making it a historic moment for the franchise. Originally announced during the September 2023 Nintendo Direct, it’s clear that Nintendo is putting considerable effort into revitalizing this RPG gem for both long-time fans and newcomers alike.

The blend of charming storytelling, innovative gameplay, and a whimsical world makes this game stand apart from other Mario games.

The game combines turn-based battles with puzzle-solving and exploration, set in a vibrant world crafted from paper. The remake promises to retain these core elements while enhancing the overall experience to match modern gaming standards.

3Tomb Raider I-III Remastered Starring Lara Croft

The “Tomb Raider I-III Remastered Starring Lara Croft” collection is set to launch on Nintendo Switch on February 14th, 2024.

This remastered collection brings the original three Tomb Raider titles, first released in 1996, into the modern era with updated visuals and more.

This release is more than just a visual upgrade. The remasters will include all of the expansions and secret levels for each game, offering a complete and enriched Tomb Raider experience.

For fans of the series and newcomers alike, this collection promises to be an exciting journey through Lara Croft’s early adventures.

The original games were groundbreaking for their time, introducing players to a new type of action-adventure gameplay and a strong, iconic female protagonist.

With this remastered collection, a new generation of gamers will have the opportunity to experience these classic games with the enhancements of modern technology.

2Mario vs. Donkey Kong

Ready to dive into a blast from the past with a modern twist? Let’s chat about the upcoming Nintendo Switch game “Mario vs. Donkey Kong,” launching on February 16, 2024.

Remember the good old 2004 Game Boy Advance game? Well, this is a complete remake of that classic, and it’s the first “Mario vs. Donkey Kong” game for the Switch, reviving the series after nearly a decade since “Mario vs. Donkey Kong: Tipping Stars”.

Alright, let’s get into the juicy details. This isn’t just any remake – it’s a total overhaul. We’re talking full 3D models instead of pre-rendered sprites, bringing the game right into the modern era of gaming.

The cutscenes? Fully animated! Imagine the charm of the original but with today’s tech. Plus, some familiar faces have gotten makeovers, like the mechanical Fire Piranha Plants and Ninjis sporting a sleek new look.

Now, the big question: What’s new in gameplay? For starters, multiplayer is now a thing! Toad joins as a second playable character, perfect for some couch co-op fun. Also, expect some tweaks in level design, like the swapping of platform locations, keeping things fresh even for the series veterans.

But let’s not forget the core of the game. As Mario, your mission is to rescue the Mini Marios from the clutches of Donkey Kong. These adorable wind-up toys are at the center of this longstanding rivalry.

And yes, Mario’s still got his wide range of moves and abilities to tackle all the puzzles and platforming challenges thrown his way.

1Dragon Quest 3 HD-2D Remake

Dragon Quest is a popular and long-running series of Japanese role-playing games (JRPGs) created by Yuji Horii and published by Square Enix (formerly Enix). The series is known for its classic turn-based gameplay, charming characters, and a rich fantasy world.

Now, when it comes to “Dragon Quest 3 HD-2D Remake”, the development of this remake is “progressing quite steadily,” as stated by Yuji Horii, the series creator. The game was announced back in May 2021 and has been eagerly anticipated ever since.

Horii himself has been closely involved, offering advice and guidance to ensure the game stays true to its roots while incorporating new elements.

This remake is described as an HD-2D version, which means you’ll get to experience the original Dragon Quest III with a unique three-dimensional feel, adding 3D effects to pixel-based backgrounds.

It’s a visual treat that blends classic and modern aesthetics, aiming to appeal to both long-time fans and newcomers.

So, are you excited about this nostalgic journey with a modern twist? It sounds like it’s going to be a fantastic experience for Dragon Quest fans and RPG lovers alike!

Keep an eye out for more updates as the release date approaches.
