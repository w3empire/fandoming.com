The Ultimate Top 10 FPS Games on the Nintendo Switch

authorLucas "ShadowStrider421" Bennett

We’ve compiled a list of the top 10 FPS games that will turn your Switch into an epic battleground.

From dystopian adventures to intergalactic skirmishes, these titles promise endless hours of excitement.

Let’s dive in!

10. Metro 2033 Redux (2020)

Embark on a gripping journey in the post-apocalyptic Moscow Metro with “Metro 2033 Redux.” This game isn’t just about shooting; it’s an immersive experience that combines survival horror, stealth, and exploration in a world where humanity clings to existence.

The Redux version enhances the original with improved graphics and smoother gameplay, ensuring a more engaging experience. You’ll navigate through dark tunnels, encounter mutated creatures, and deal with hostile factions.

The game’s atmosphere is its real star, with eerie lighting and sound design that pull you deep into its haunting narrative. Plus, the moral choices you make impact the story’s outcome, adding a layer of depth to your actions.

Get ready to ration your ammo and filter supply – survival is a real challenge here!

9. Crysis Remastered Trilogy (2021)

Crysis, a name synonymous with jaw-dropping graphics, brings its Remastered Trilogy to the Switch. This collection includes “Crysis,” “Crysis 2,” and “Crysis 3,” each offering a unique blend of sandbox-style combat and a sci-fi narrative.

The first game sets the stage with its iconic Nanosuit, allowing you to adapt to different combat scenarios. The second and third installments up the ante with urban warfare and a dramatic storyline.

The remastered versions maintain the series’ visual excellence while being impressively optimized for the Switch. Whether you’re cloaking to sneak past enemies or going all guns blazing, the Crysis series offers a versatile FPS experience.

And let’s not forget the satisfaction of hearing “Maximum Armor” as you take down hordes of alien invaders!

8. Paladins (2019)

“Paladins” stands out with its unique blend of FPS and hero shooter elements. This free-to-play game boasts a diverse roster of characters, each with their own unique abilities and playstyles. Whether you prefer sniping from afar or diving into the fray, there’s a champion for you.

The game’s charm lies in its strategic team play and customization options. With various loadouts and talents, you can tailor your favorite champions to suit your playstyle.

The vibrant graphics and whimsical art style add to its appeal, making it a welcoming title for both seasoned shooters and newcomers.

Regular updates, new champions, and maps keep the gameplay fresh, ensuring that “Paladins” remains an addictive and fun experience on the Switch.

7. Bright Memory: Infinite Gold Edition (2022)

“Bright Memory: Infinite Gold Edition” is a visual masterpiece on the Switch, offering a fusion of FPS and action-adventure.

Set in a futuristic world, you play as Shelia, an agent tasked with stopping a military organization from awakening a dead ancient power. The game stands out for its fluid combat system, allowing you to seamlessly switch between firearms, a sword, and supernatural abilities.

The fast-paced action is complemented by stunning environments, from ancient ruins to futuristic cities. Although it’s a shorter experience compared to other titles on this list, “Bright Memory: Infinite” packs a punch with its intense battles and breathtaking visuals.

It’s a perfect pick for those looking for a cinematic FPS experience on the go.

6. Borderlands: The Handsome Collection (2020)

“Borderlands: The Handsome Collection” brings the beloved looter-shooter franchise to the Switch in grand style. This collection includes “Borderlands 2” and “Borderlands: The Pre-Sequel,” offering hundreds of hours of gameplay.

Known for its unique art style, hilarious writing, and an insane array of guns, the Borderlands series is a joyride from start to finish.

Each game features a diverse set of characters, each with unique abilities and skill trees. The real fun, however, lies in the co-op gameplay, where you and your friends can team up for some chaotic shoot-and-loot action.

The collection’s optimization for the Switch is impressive, maintaining smooth performance even in the most explosive of battles. If you’re looking for a mix of humor, action, and endless loot, this collection is a no-brainer.

5. Call of Juarez: Gunslinger (2019)

“Call of Juarez: Gunslinger” takes you back to the Wild West, offering a refreshing change of pace from the usual futuristic or modern-day shooters.

You step into the boots of Silas Greaves, a bounty hunter recounting his tales of hunting the most notorious outlaws. The game’s narrative is cleverly woven into the gameplay, with events and environments changing as Silas embellishes his stories.

The shooting mechanics are satisfying, with a focus on quick reflexes and sharpshooting. The game’s dueling system is a standout feature, testing your patience and timing in intense one-on-one showdowns. The cel-shaded graphics give it a distinct, comic-book style, further enhancing its charm.

For those who fancy a dose of nostalgia and some old-school shooting, “Call of Juarez: Gunslinger” is a must-play.

4. Neon White (2022)

“Neon White” is a unique blend of FPS and speedrunning, offering a fast-paced and addictive experience. You play as White, an assassin from Hell competing against other demon slayers for a chance to live in Heaven.

The game’s core mechanic involves using “soul cards” that grant weapons or special abilities, which can also be discarded for movement powers. This creates a fascinating dynamic where you must constantly decide between offense and agility.

The levels are short but designed for replayability as you strive to beat your best times. The anime-inspired visuals and energetic soundtrack add to the game’s high-octane feel.

“Neon White” is perfect for those who love the thrill of speed and precision, making every second and every shot count.

3. Turok 2: Seeds of Evil (2019)

“Turok 2: Seeds of Evil” is a classic FPS that has been wonderfully remastered for the Nintendo Switch. This game takes you back to the late ’90s with its dinosaur-hunting adventure but with modern improvements like enhanced graphics and controls.

As Turok, you are tasked with defeating the Primagen, a powerful alien who threatens the universe. The game is known for its challenging gameplay, intricate levels, and an arsenal of unique weapons (who can forget the Cerebral Bore?).

The remaster makes navigating these levels and battling diverse enemies more enjoyable than ever. It’s a nostalgic trip for those who played the original and a fresh discovery for newcomers to the series.

2. Wolfenstein 2: The New Colossus (2018)

“Wolfenstein 2: The New Colossus” brings its high-octane action and compelling narrative to the Switch. This game continues the story of B.J. Blazkowicz as he fights against a Nazi-occupied America.

The game balances intense gunfights with a gripping storyline, filled with memorable characters and moments.

The shooting mechanics are solid, with a satisfying array of weapons and abilities at your disposal. What sets it apart is its cinematic storytelling, delivering a narrative that’s both emotional and exhilarating.

The Switch version does an admirable job of translating this experience to a handheld format, making it a fantastic choice for those who appreciate a good story with their action.

1. Apex Legends (2021)

“Apex Legends” dives into the world of battle royale with its fast-paced, squad-based gameplay. This free-to-play title stands out with its unique roster of “Legends,” each with distinct abilities and playstyles.

The game emphasizes teamwork and strategy, with each match offering a different experience based on your squad’s composition and tactics.

The dynamic map, diverse weapons, and the thrill of being the last team standing make each game a heart-pounding experience. Apex Legends on the Switch maintains the core gameplay experience, with adjustments to accommodate the platform.

It’s a testament to the game’s design that it feels just as engaging and competitive on a handheld device. For fans of battle royale and tactical team play, Apex Legends is a top pick.
