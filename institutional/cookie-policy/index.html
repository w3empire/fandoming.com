What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work or work in a more efficient way, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

Use of Cookies by Our Site

Third-Party Cookies

While Fandoming currently does not use first-party cookies (cookies set directly by our Site), our Site does use the following third-party services, which may use cookies:

  • Google AdSense: This service displays ads on our Site. It uses cookies to make the ads more relevant to users and to prevent the same ad from being shown repeatedly.
  • Google Search API: This service enhances the search functionality on our Site.

These third-party services may place cookies on your device for the purposes outlined in their respective privacy policies.

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences by setting or amending your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website, though your access to some functionality and areas of our website may be restricted.
