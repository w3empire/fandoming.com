How Affiliate Marketing Works

Affiliate marketing is a marketing arrangement in which an online retailer pays a commission to an external website for traffic or sales generated from its referrals. We participate in affiliate marketing programs, which means we may earn a commission when you click on or make purchases via affiliate links.

Amazon Affiliate Program

Currently, Fandoming is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com. As part of this program, we post customized links, provided by Amazon, to track the referrals to their website. This program utilizes cookies to track visits for the purposes of assigning commissions on these sales.

Impartiality and Integrity

Our participation in the Amazon Affiliate Program does not influence our content. The opinions, reviews, and recommendations we provide on our site are always independent, genuine, and based on our editorial standards and values. Our priority is to provide valuable and honest information to our community.

Products and services are recommended because we believe they are of interest to our audience, not because of the small commissions we make if you decide to buy something through our affiliate links.

Remember, you are under no obligation to purchase any products through our affiliate links. If you choose to do so, we appreciate your support and thank you for contributing to the growth and sustainability of Fandoming.
