Privacy Policy for Fandoming.com

Last Updated:: 2023-12-20

Welcome to Fandoming! Protecting your privacy is crucial to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we, at Fandoming, collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website “fandoming.com”.

We reserve the right to make changes to this policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the “Last Updated” date of this Privacy Policy. You are encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates.

1. Collection of Your Information

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the site includes:

Personal Data: Personally identifiable information, such as your name and email address, that you voluntarily give to us when you choose to participate in various activities related to the site, such as online chat and message boards.

Derived Data: Information our servers automatically collect when you access the site, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and the pages you have viewed directly before and after accessing the site.

2. Use of Cookies and Web Beacons

We do not currently use first-party cookies on our website, but this may change in the future. However, third-party services such as Google Analytics, Google AdSense, and Google Search API used on our site may use cookies and web beacons to collect information.

This information is used to, among other things, analyze and track data, determine the popularity of certain content, and better understand online activity.

Third-Party Services:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how our website is being used in order to improve the user experience. Google Analytics may collect information such as your IP address, web browser type, and pages visited on our site.
  • Google AdSense: We display ads through Google AdSense. Google AdSense may use cookies to show personalized ads based on your previous visits to our site and other sites on the internet.
  • Google Search API: This service is used to enhance search functionality on our website.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, please visit Google’s Privacy & Terms.

3. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites that are not affiliated with us. Once you have used these links to leave our site, any information you provide to these third parties is not covered by this Privacy Policy.

4. Media

  • All media on our site, including images, videos, and other forms of media, is used in the context of reviews or to create transformative content with our opinions and added commentary.
  • We strictly adhere to the fair use clause under local copyright law for all copyrighted media on our site.

5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information.

While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.

6. Policy for Children

We do not knowingly solicit information from or market to children under the age of 13. If you become aware of any data we have collected from children under the age of 13, please contact us.

7. Controls for Do-Not-Track Features

Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems include a Do-Not-Track (“DNT”) feature or setting you can activate to signal your privacy preference not to have data about your online browsing activities monitored and collected.

8. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will update the Privacy Policy modification date above.
